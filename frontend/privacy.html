privacy policy
last updated: April 19, 2026  ·  applies to asterisk.authsrng.xyz and self-hosted instances
overview
asterisk is an open-source, self-hosted chat platform. this policy covers the instance operated at asterisk.authsrng.xyz. if you run your own instance, you are the data controller for your users.

we take privacy seriously and collect only what is strictly necessary to run the service. we do not sell, share, or monetize your data. there are no ads.

eligibility
age requirement

asterisk is intended for users 14 years of age or older. by creating an account you confirm that you meet this requirement. if we discover that a user is under 14, we will delete the account and associated data.

if you believe a user under 14 has registered, please contact us so we can remove the account promptly.

data we collect
account & profile data
data details
username chosen by you at registration. visible to other users. 2–20 characters, letters/numbers/_-.
password stored as a bcrypt hash. we never store or transmit your plaintext password.
color a randomly assigned display color (HSL value). used to distinguish your messages.
bio optional, up to 160 characters. set and edited by you. visible to other users.
status optional presence status (online, away, busy, invisible). visible to other users while connected.
auth token a JWT stored in your browser's localStorage. used to keep you logged in. does not contain sensitive info beyond your username and color.
messages & content
data details
channel messages messages sent in #global, #debate, #gaming, and #music are stored on the server. up to 30,000 messages per channel are retained.
direct messages DMs between friends are stored on the server. up to 5,000 messages per conversation are retained.
node messages messages sent inside nodes (private group chats) are stored per-channel on the server.
reactions emoji reactions (and which users added them) are stored alongside messages.
file uploads images, videos, and text files you upload are stored on the server. accepted types: JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP, MP4, WebM, Ogg, plain text. max size: 8 MB.
social data
data details
friend list accepted friendships, pending requests you sent, and requests you received are stored on the server.
nodes nodes you own or belong to, including membership lists, invite codes, and ban lists, are stored on the server.
local / browser data
data details
settings appearance preferences (accent color, font size, density, notification settings, etc.) are stored in your browser's localStorage. they never leave your device.
gif favourites GIFs you favourite are stored in your browser's localStorage only. we do not sync these to the server.
seen releases the tag of the last release banner you dismissed is stored in your browser's localStorage to avoid repeated popups.
what we don't collect

we do not collect or store any of the following:

✓ no IP address logs  ✓ no email address  ✓ no real name  ✓ no phone number  ✓ no payment data  ✓ no analytics or tracking  ✓ no ads

we do not use cookies. we do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking pixels.

third-party services
GIPHY

the GIF picker uses the GIPHY API proxied through our server. when you search for or send a GIF, a request is made to GIPHY's servers via our backend. your search query is passed to GIPHY. GIPHY's own privacy policy applies to this data. we do not pass your username or any identifying information to GIPHY.

GitHub API

on login, the app checks the GitHub releases API for the latest asterisk version to display a release banner. this request goes from your browser directly to api.github.com. no personal data is sent. GitHub's privacy policy applies.

Google Fonts

the app loads the JetBrains Mono font from Google Fonts. this means Google's servers receive a request from your browser containing your IP address and browser info. Google's privacy policy applies. if you self-host, you may serve the font locally instead.

how we use your data

data is used solely to operate the chat service: authenticating your account, delivering messages to the right people, displaying usernames and profile info, and enforcing rate limits. we do not use your data for any other purpose.

data retention & deletion

messages are retained up to the limits described above (30,000 per channel, 5,000 per DM conversation). older messages are automatically removed as new ones arrive.

to delete your account and all associated data, contact us. upon deletion we will remove your account record, your messages, and your DMs from our servers as soon as practicable.

uploaded files are stored indefinitely until the account is deleted or a server administrator removes them manually.

security

passwords are hashed with bcrypt (cost factor 10) and never stored in plaintext. authentication uses signed JWT tokens. connections are made over HTTPS/WSS when the server is configured with TLS.

that said, no system is perfectly secure. do not share sensitive personal information in messages.

self-hosted instances

asterisk is open-source (MIT license). if you run your own instance, you are responsible for the privacy of your users. this policy only covers the instance at asterisk.authsrng.xyz.

changes to this policy

we may update this policy from time to time. when we do, the "last updated" date at the top will change. continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
